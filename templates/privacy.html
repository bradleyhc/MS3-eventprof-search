Privacy

Privacy on this site

This website is solely for demonstration purposes. Whilst users are able to provide their details during registration or editing their profile, there is no requirement that this data is accurate. Users must not however, upload any data or images of other, 'real' people e.g users must not enter an active email of others, without their prior consent.

If it is found that a user does not genuinely own an active email that has been registered, the email owner will be notified and the profile on EventProf Search permanently deleted

Data we collect

Data is only collected on this site for the purposes of site demonstration and account creation. Whilst true data is not required, we ask for the following sensitive information upon account creation:

  • Email address

  • Name

  • Location

  • Profile Image

With the exception of Email, all data is used to display on your profile for other users to see. Your email is used for the creation of the account, and as the 'reply to' email when you send users an email via the 'contact' button. Your email will remain hidden to users who send you a message via this function, unless you reply directly to them.

You cannot update your email address, but can request that it be changed by emailing the site owner.

Session data

When you login or register, we generate a new browser session containing only the details required to deliver the site functionality to you. This includes:

  • User slug (your first and last name merged)

  • Your user type

  • Your admin status

When you logout from EventProf Search, this session data is destroyed, and a new session created each time you login thereafter.

Access and deleting my data

As a user of this site, you can request the information stored in the database pertaining to your account, or request that your data be permanently deleted. To request this, simply simply email us.
